Meet the Team

Ronmexico

Guild Master

As the determined leader of The Dirty Dogs, Ronmexico has guided the guild to numerous victories in the World of Warcraft Classic. Known for his tactical prowess and unwavering focus, he is a master of controlling demonic forces and bending them to his will. With his trusty imp companion, Sparky, by his side, Ronmexico is always devising new strategies to conquer the toughest challenges in Azeroth.

Ferfax

Raid leader

Ferfax, the versatile and adaptive Raid Leader, is a Night Elf Druid deeply connected with the forces of nature. Hailing from the dense forests of Teldrassil, Ferfax is known for his ability to switch between the ferocious Cat Form to deal devastating damage and the resilient Bear Form to endure enemy attacks. With a deep understanding of his fellow raiders' abilities, Ferfax expertly coordinates his team to tackle the most formidable foes of Azeroth.

Bresus

Loot Master

Bresus, the Loot Master of The Dirty Dogs, is a Gnome Mage with an insatiable passion for all things fiery. From the snowy peaks of Dun Morogh, Bresus has spent countless hours perfecting his craft, mastering the art of fire magic to obliterate his enemies. As the one responsible for distributing the spoils of war, Bresus takes great pride in ensuring every member of the guild is fairly rewarded for their hard-fought victories.

Jacobdegrom

Human Resources

Jacobdegrom, the compassionate Human Resources manager, is a Human Shadow Priest with a deep connection to the shadowy realm of the Void. With her innate ability to tap into this mysterious power, Jacobdegrom can unleash devastating shadow magic to vanquish her foes. When she's not commanding the darkness, she is the heart and soul of The Dirty Dogs, ensuring the guild remains a welcoming and supportive environment for all its members.
