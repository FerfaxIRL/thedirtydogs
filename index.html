The Dirty Dogs - WoW Classic Guild

Meet The Team

Check out our accomplishments on Warcraft Logs.

Ronmexico

Guild Master

As the determined leader of The Dirty Dogs, Ronmexico has guided the guild to numerous victories in the World of Warcraft Classic. Known for his tactical prowess and unwavering focus, he is a master of controlling demonic forces and bending them to his will. With his trusty imp companion, Zormat, by his side, Ronmexico is always devising new strategies to conquer the toughest challenges in Azeroth.

Ferfax

Raid leader

Ferfax, the versatile and adaptive Raid Leader, is a Night Elf Druid deeply connected with the forces of nature. Hailing from the dense forests of Teldrassil, Ferfax is known for his ability to switch between the ferocious Cat Form to deal devastating damage and the resilient Bear Form to endure enemy attacks. With a deep understanding of his fellow raiders' abilities, Ferfax expertly coordinates his team to tackle the most formidable foes of Azeroth.

Bresus

Loot Master

Bresus, the Loot Master of The Dirty Dogs, is a Gnome Mage with an insatiable passion for all things fiery. From the snowy peaks of Dun Morogh, Bresus has spent countless hours perfecting his craft, mastering the art of fire magic to obliterate his enemies. As the one responsible for distributing the spoils of war, Bresus takes great pride in ensuring every member of the guild is fairly rewarded for their hard-fought victories.

Jacobdegrom

Human Resources

Jacobdegrom, the compassionate Human Resources manager, is a Human Shadow Priest with a deep connection to the shadowy realm of the Void. With her innate ability to tap into this mysterious power, Jacobdegrom can unleash devastating shadow magic to vanquish her foes. When she's not commanding the darkness, she is the heart and soul of The Dirty Dogs, ensuring the guild remains a welcoming and supportive environment for all its members.

Gatbot

Gatbot, a daring and resourceful hunter, is always ready for the thrill of the hunt. In his downtime, he can be found at the strip club, unwinding and socializing with friends. With his trusty pet by his side, Gatbot never shies away from a challenge, always pushing himself and the guild to new heights.

Sonandy

Sonandy, the stalwart Blood Deathknight, is the backbone of the guild's raids. As the main tank, he keeps the enemies at bay and maintains control over the battlefield. A true role-playing enthusiast, Sonandy is always ready to immerse himself in the rich world of Azeroth, adding depth and excitement to every guild event.

Hottsalot

Hottsalot is a caring and nurturing Restoration Druid who excels at keeping the team alive through even the most challenging encounters. With a keen eye for detail and a perpetual sense of curiosity, Hottsalot is always questioning the source of incoming damage and seeking ways to improve the guild's performance.

Blocparty

Blocparty, the fiery Fire Mage, is a beloved member of the guild, known for his friendly demeanor and camaraderie. His blazing spells can turn the tide of battle and light up the darkest dungeons. Always ready with a kind word or a helping hand, Blocparty is an indispensable part of The Dirty Dogs family.

Uwuther

Uwuther is a dedicated Holy Paladin who brings light and healing to his comrades in battle. When he's not saving lives on the front lines, Uwuther can be found indulging in his love for gambling and sharing the latest memes with his guildmates. His unique blend of humor and skill make him a valued and cherished member of the team.
